Resource Profile: eICR Anonymized Practitioner

Official URL: http://fhir.org/fhir/us/anonymized-eicr/StructureDefinition/eicr-anon-practitioner Version: 1.0.0
Active as of 2024-06-28 Computable Name: EICRAnonymizedPractitioner

This Practitioner profile represents an anonymized eCR Practitioner.

It is based on the US Core Practitioner profile with further constraints to require masking of:

  • name
  • telecom
  • address

Usage:

  • Refer to this Resource Profile: eICR Anonymized Composition and eICR Anonymized PractitionerRole
  • Examples for this Resource Profile: Practitioner/practitioner-anon
